About Us

Together We Can Make a Difference

We are dedicated to bringing hope, support, and opportunities to communities in need.

AGABHA HOPE LADIES FOUNDATION is a Christian non-profit organization committed to empowering girls and women through education, mentorship, and sustainable development programs.

Our Vision

To build a generation of healthy, confident, and god-fearing girls by meeting their physical, emotional, and spiritual needs.

Our Mission

To demonstrate the love of Christ by empowering girls through menstrual dignity, mentoring young people, and bringing hope to vulnerable families through compassionate services.

Reusable Sanitary Towels

Empowering Girls Through Dignity

Our Reusable Sanitary Towels program provides sustainable menstrual hygiene solutions to girls in underserved communities. Each girl receives a kit containing high-quality reusable pads, hygiene education, and ongoing support.

"Each girl receives a high-quality reusable sanitary towel that lasts up to 2 years."

"We use soft, durable, and washable fabrics that are comfortable for young girls."

"Our team of skilled women tailors handcraft each towel with love and precision."

"The workshop is a place of skill-building, fellowship, and empowerment."

"Every kit is carefully packaged with care and a prayer before distribution."

Impact: 95% of girls improved school attendance and confidence.

Girls Mentorship

Building Tomorrow's Leaders

Our Girls Mentorship program pairs young girls with trained mentors who guide them through life's challenges, building confidence and leadership skills.

"In a safe and cozy environment, girls learn life skills and build lasting friendships."

Testimonial: "My mentor believed in me when I didn't believe in myself. Now I'm in university studying to become a doctor." - Mary, 19

Children's Bible Ministry

Sharing God's Love With Children

Our Children's Bible Ministry creates a safe and joyful environment where children learn about God's love through engaging Bible stories, songs, games, and prayer sessions.

  • Engaging Bible stories and lessons
  • Fun songs, games, and activities
  • Memory verse challenges
  • Prayer and worship sessions
Impact: Over 300 children baptized and actively involved in local churches.
