Reusable Sanitary Towels

Empowering Girls Through Dignity

Our Reusable Sanitary Towels program provides sustainable menstrual hygiene solutions to girls in underserved communities. Each girl receives a kit containing:

  • High-quality reusable pads that last up to 2 years
  • Hygiene education and training
  • Safe disposal and washing facilities
  • Ongoing support and mentorship
Impact: 95% of girls improved school attendance.

Girls Mentorship

Building Tomorrow's Leaders

Our Girls Mentorship program pairs young girls with trained mentors who guide them through life's challenges.

  • Leadership development and confidence
  • Career guidance and education support
  • Life skills training
  • Emotional and spiritual wellness
Testimonial: "My mentor believed in me when I didn't believe in myself." - Mary
