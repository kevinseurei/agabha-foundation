Reusable Sanitary Towels

Empowering Girls Through Dignity

Our Reusable Sanitary Towels program provides sustainable menstrual hygiene solutions to girls in underserved communities. Each girl receives a kit containing:

  • High-quality reusable sanitary pads that last up to 2 years
  • Hygiene education and training on proper use and care
  • Access to safe disposal and washing facilities
  • Ongoing support and mentorship

This program keeps girls in school, reduces absenteeism, and restores their dignity. Since 2022, we've distributed over 2,000 kits across 50+ schools in Kenya.

Impact: 95% of girls who received our kits reported improved school attendance and confidence.

Girls Mentorship

Building Tomorrow's Leaders

Our Girls Mentorship program pairs young girls with trained mentors who guide them through life's challenges and opportunities. The program focuses on:

  • Leadership development and confidence building
  • Career guidance and educational support
  • Life skills training and decision-making
  • Emotional and spiritual wellness

Through weekly group sessions and one-on-one mentoring, we've helped over 500 girls discover their potential and pursue their dreams.

Testimonial: "My mentor believed in me when I didn't believe in myself. Now I'm in university studying to become a doctor." - Mary, 19

Children's Bible Ministry

Sharing God's Love With Children

Our Children's Bible Ministry creates a safe and joyful environment where children learn about God's love through:

  • Engaging Bible stories and lessons
  • Fun songs, games, and creative activities
  • Memory verse challenges and prizes
  • Prayer and worship sessions

We reach over 1,000 children annually through our weekly programs, holiday camps, and community outreach events. Many children have made decisions to follow Christ and grow in their faith.

Impact: Over 300 children have been baptized and are actively involved in their local churches.
